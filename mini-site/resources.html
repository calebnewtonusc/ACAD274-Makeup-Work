Glossary (Consolidated)

Incorporated place
A city/town/borough/municipality with its own local government; figures reflect residents within city limits.
Population estimate
An annual official count of residents used for inter‑year comparisons.
Population density
Residents per unit land area (per square mile for U.S.; per square kilometre for Canada).
Population Centre (Canada)
A built‑up area used for census statistics; distinct from a full metropolitan area.
