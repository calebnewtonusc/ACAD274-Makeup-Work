Glossary

Population Centre
A built-up area used for census stats; different from a full metro area.
Land Area
Size of the centre’s land in square kilometres (km²).
Population Density
Residents per km² (population ÷ land area).
Change %
Percent change from 2016 to 2021 populations.
Core Column
The main column used for sorting/comparison (2021 Population here).
