Two Custom Visualizations Supporting Policy Recommendations

Visualization 1: Optimal New Facility Placement Strategy

Location: Recommendations → Facility Placement Section

Purpose: Identify and visualize the 10 highest-priority locations for new healthcare facilities based on geospatial analysis of access deserts, population density, and socioeconomic need.

Implementation:

  • Uses KD-tree nearest-neighbor algorithm with CA State Plane projection (EPSG:2229)
  • Interactive Leaflet.js map with priority markers color-coded by urgency
  • Calculated optimal placement to maximize population coverage while minimizing average access distance
  • Incorporates census tract boundaries, facility locations, and population density overlays
  • Priority ranking based on composite score: population served, current distance, median income, uninsured rate

Methodology: KD-tree algorithm calculates nearest facility for each census tract. Priority sites selected by identifying areas with highest population × distance product, filtered by socioeconomic vulnerability indicators.

Visualization 2: Policy Intervention ROI Projection (10-Year Timeline)

Location: Recommendations → Financial Analysis Section

Purpose: Demonstrate the projected return on investment (ROI) and population impact of recommended healthcare facility interventions over a 10-year implementation timeline.

Implementation:

  • Built with Chart.js for animated timeline visualization
  • Dual-axis chart showing cumulative ROI (%) and residents served over time
  • Phased implementation model: Years 1-2 (Initial), Years 3-5 (Expansion), Years 6-10 (Full Coverage)
  • Financial modeling incorporates facility construction costs, operational expenses, and healthcare cost savings
  • Population impact calculated using access improvement × utilization rate × preventive care benefit

Financial Model: $645.3M total investment over 10 years. Savings from reduced ER visits, improved preventive care access, and better health outcomes yield $3.48B in total healthcare cost savings. Break-even at Year 3, 540% cumulative ROI by Year 10.
